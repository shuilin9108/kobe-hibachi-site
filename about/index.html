About Kobe Hibachi Catering

Bringing the authentic hibachi restaurant experience to your backyard since day one

Our Passion for Hibachi

At Kobe Hibachi Catering, we believe that great food should come with an unforgettable experience. What started as a love for authentic Japanese hibachi cooking has grown into New York's most sought-after private hibachi chef service.

We specialize in bringing the full hibachi restaurant experience directly to your home — the sizzling grill, the mesmerizing fire tricks, the impressive knife skills, and of course, the mouthwatering flavors that keep guests coming back for more.

Whether it's a birthday celebration in Staten Island, a graduation party in Brooklyn, or a corporate event in Manhattan, our professional chefs arrive with everything needed to transform your backyard into a premium hibachi dining experience.

What Sets Us Apart

We don't just cook food — we create memories. Every Kobe Hibachi event is a full-sensory experience that combines culinary artistry with live entertainment. Our chefs are trained in traditional teppanyaki techniques and bring the energy, showmanship, and skill that make hibachi dining so special.

From the moment our chef arrives to the final cleanup, we handle everything. You and your guests simply sit back, watch the show, and enjoy the freshest ingredients grilled right before your eyes.

We pride ourselves on using only premium, fresh-sourced ingredients — from tender filet mignon and juicy shrimp to crisp vegetables and perfectly seasoned fried rice. Every dish is prepared with care, passion, and a whole lot of fire.
